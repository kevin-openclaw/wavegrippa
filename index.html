The Story

Producer. DJ. wavegrippa.

wavegrippa is an electronic music producer and multi-genre DJ from the UK. Started making tunes in a bedroom (as is tradition), graduated to making tunes in a slightly bigger room with better monitors.

The sound? Hard to pin down. Could be deep rolling house one minute, chunky DnB the next….funk, modern soul, big beats, 80's dancehall or a cheeky garage bootleg thrown in for good measure. It's basically a mixed bag of nuts.
